# Genology

## Overview

Genology, (Teyvat Language: "<span lang="Teyvat"> Genology</span>") is the second set of books launched by the *Teyvat Insitute of Culture and Science*. This work delves deeply into the fundamental rules, historical changes, and world structure of the Teyvat universe, providing readers with a comprehensive opportunity to understand this fantastical world. "Genology" has become the authoritative guide to exploring the world of Teyvat with its rigorous academic approach and rich content.

## Outline

"Genology" is divided into multiple chapters, each focusing on different aspects of the Teyvat world:

1. **Chapter 1: Basic Rules of Teyvat**
   * Introduction to commonly used consumables such as currency and resin
   * Detailed explanation of character development systems, including artifacts and weapons
   * Explanation of game systems like domains and wishes
   * Exploration of elemental reaction mechanisms
2. **Chapter 2: Historical Changes in Teyvat**
   * A chronological and rigorous narration of significant historical events
   * Each section uses a time interval as the main title
   * Focus on verified historical information, with careful treatment of uncertain content
3. **Chapter 3: The World Structure of Teyvat**
   * Detailed introduction to the geography and cultural features of each nation/region
   * Analysis of governance methods and power institutions of each nation
   * Compilation of known deities' information
   * Explanation of the hierarchical power structure in Teyvat
   * Overview of the known elemental dragons' status

This book is currently in the process of being written and is applying for secondary creation products with miHoYo.
