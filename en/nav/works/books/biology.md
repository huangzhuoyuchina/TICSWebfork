# Teyvat Biology

## General Introduction
The *Teyvat Biology* is a comprehensive document that records the biodiversity of the Teyvat continent. It provides a detailed depiction of the rich variety of life in Teyvat, from tiny insects to complex plant and animal communities, covering all aspects of Teyvat's biological world. This chronicle is not only a faithful record of Teyvat's natural ecology but also an important reference for biological science research.

## Expected Content
The *Teyvat Biology* records essential biological data and also describes Teyvat's unique biogeographical distribution and ecosystem characteristics in detail. The book is divided into chapters based on the countries and regions of Teyvat, ranging from vast forests to rich water bodies, from high mountain meadows to lowland wetlands. Teyvat's diverse natural environments have fostered a wide variety of biological species, which play important roles in maintaining ecological balance and biodiversity within their ecosystems.
